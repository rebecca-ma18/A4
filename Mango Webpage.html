    About Mango

    Mango is a 1 year old Cockapoo. He loves to socialize with people and other dogs, having a great time! Mango likes to look outside where the squirrels and birds may be, wanting to chase them even though he knows he shouldn’t be. He knows a handful of tricks and will do all of them for a treat. He also loves his toys and will play with them for a majority of his day.
